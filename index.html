Husky Wushu

About

The Husky Wushu Team is a student organization at the University of Washington that practices and promotes Wushu, a form of contemporary Chinese martial arts. Throughout the year, we practice, perform demonstrations, and compete at various events. Our team comprises of students from different experience levels, ages, and backgrounds. We welcome all who want to stop by and practice with us. 

Train

FALL QUARTER PRACTICE TIMES: 8:30 PM Mondays and Wednesdays in IMA Studio 316. 3:30-5:30 PM on Fridays in IMA Mat Room B. NOTE: There is no practice this Friday Sep. 30 due to IMA Closure at 1:30 PM.

Demonstrate

We perform Wushu Demonstrations throughout the year at events such as CSA's Lunar New Year, and Sakura-Con! 

Compete

We compete at events such as the Chinese Martial Arts Tournament, Collegiate Wushu Tournament, and Seattle International Martial Arts Championship.

Contact Us

Send us a message! Let us know if you want to drop by a practice!
